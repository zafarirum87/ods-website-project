Our Services

The customer experience is at the heart of our motivations. Although we mainly work with designers, architects and other industry professionals, we regularly work directly with the client for one-off projects. We support you throughout your project.

measurements

We will travel to the site to take an accurate record of the measurements necessary for the production of shop drawings.

Workshop drawings

We produce shop drawings accompanied by 3D modeling. This step allows us to ensure that our understanding of the project matches the client's expectations. These drawings are also the link to the manufacturing.

Manufacturing

We are specialized in the manufacture of high-end custom residential, commercial and institutional furniture. Custom-made kitchens, bathroom furniture, custom-made cupboards and storage.

Finish

All the finishing steps are done on site in our workshops in our two painting rooms by our team of trained painters.

Installation

Since a project is only completed once it has taken root in its environment, we offer an installation service in the Oakly Homes region and its surroundings by our qualified workforce for a perfect integration into your space.
